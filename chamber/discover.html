History of Gridley

In the 1850s a wool grower by the name of Geroge W. Gridley settled in in the area. He owned over 960-acres. Gridley was established in 1870 when the Oregon and California Railroad was being contrustuted. In 2018 during the large Camp fire that was in Paradise, California, Gridley had become a place where many suerviors of the fire were able to stay. Then in 2020 a former NASA engineer and Youtuber named Mark Rober succeded in beating the World's Largest Elephant's Toothpaste Explosion with the hight of 60 feet.
